Our Story

The name JIKU was born from the Japanese words 'Ji' = time and 'Ku' = Space, which is the essence of 3D human movement. Jiku Stats has been in development over the last 15 years, and recently validated in the Journal of Statistical Software.

​With over 1,000 downloads of the Jiku Stats beta platform, we are now market ready to introduce Jiku Stats to the health & fitness fields to help advance the human movement innovation gap that has existed in this field for the past 50 years.

Our Vision

By introducing Jiku Stats to the health & fitness fields, we aim to offer an open-source core platform to better serve the human movement analytics market. This will fill a notable market gap within the human movement sciences.

Our platform offers a new form of human movement analysis within the med-tech field, which is a notable market gap within all other current statistical platforms. We believe Jiku Stats will improve the standard of human movement analysis, with will lead to efficient, effective and novel innovations within the fields of the health & fitness.
